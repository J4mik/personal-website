About me

I am a young programmer and engineer with a passion for 3D printing and electonics. In my free time I like to work on projects that combine my different interests and teach me new knowledge.
Recently I have been working a filament dryer for my 3D printer. I made it using a cheap header from Aliexpress as well as an Arduino nano, lcd screen, rotary encoder and discreate component like transistors soldered together on a breadboard.
